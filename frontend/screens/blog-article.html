10 Essential Tips for Sustainable Home Organization

Sarah Miller

Eco-Living Consultant • 5 min read

Published Oct 24, 2023

"Sustainable organization isn't just about labels and bins; it's about choosing products that respect our planet while simplifying our daily routines."

In today's fast-paced world, finding peace at home starts with organization. Using sustainable materials from your local droguerie not only helps the environment but creates a timeless aesthetic that plastic bins simply cannot match.

1. Embrace Glass Over Plastic

Glass jars are the backbone of any eco-friendly pantry. They are infinitely recyclable, non-porous, and allow you to see exactly what you have on hand. Visit our shop to find apothecary-style jars that bring a vintage charm to your kitchen shelves.

2. The Magic of Bamboo Brushes

Cleaning tools are often overlooked in home organization. Replacing synthetic sponges with bamboo dish brushes and natural fiber scrubbers makes your cleaning station look organized and professional.

When everything has a dedicated place, the mental load of maintaining a household significantly decreases. Start by decluttering one room at a time, focusing on high-traffic areas like the laundry room or the entryway.
