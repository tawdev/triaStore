Our Story

Crafting better homes and stronger communities since 1995 through quality hardware and expert care.

From a Humble Workbench

Founded nearly three decades ago, Droguerie began as a small corner shop dedicated to helping neighbors fix what was broken and build what was imagined. What started with a single shelf of hand tools has grown into a cornerstone of the community.

Our philosophy is simple: provide the highest quality products alongside the kind of expert advice you can only get from someone who's spent a lifetime on the job. We believe that every home improvement project, no matter how small, deserves the best materials.

Today, we continue that legacy, blending traditional service with modern convenience to ensure you have everything you need to create a space you love.
